## Goal

Lift the beta gate. Public launch. Marketing push. Yakima realtor association outreach.

## Tasks

1. **Attorney pass.** Final legal review of Terms, Privacy Policy, Realtor Agreement, Vendor Agreement, Acceptable Use, Cookie Policy. Engage WA-licensed attorney. Document any required changes in `docs/legal/`.
2. **Press kit.** `docs/launch/PRESS-KIT.md` already exists — refresh with current numbers from beta. Include:
   - One-paragraph platform description
   - Three-paragraph long-form description
   - Hi-res logos (light + dark)
   - Three founder photos
   - Quote from founder
   - Beta milestone numbers + testimonials (with permission)
3. **Soft launch.** Lift beta gate. Anyone can sign up. Announce on:
   - Yakima Valley Realtor Association Slack/Facebook
   - LinkedIn post from founder
   - Email to beta participants asking them to share
4. **Hard launch (week 2).** Local press outreach:
   - Yakima Herald-Republic
   - Yakima Valley Business Times
   - KIMA / KAPP / KVEW local TV (real estate segment pitches)
   - Pacific Northwest Real Estate Magazine
5. **Realtor association outreach.** Founder presents at the YVRA monthly meeting. Demo, Q&A, signup booth.
6. **Vendor outreach.** Founder personally walks 20 local vendors through onboarding. Goal: launch with 50+ active vendors across the category tree.
7. **Post-launch monitoring.** First 30 days:
   - Daily check on signups, AI tool usage, lead conversion
   - Weekly retro on growth + product issues
   - Quarterly NPS survey
8. **Public launch success criteria** in `docs/launch/LAUNCH-CHECKLIST.md`:
   - 500 signups in first month
   - 100+ posts published
   - 200+ marketplace inquiries
   - 50+ active vendors
   - Lighthouse + axe + Sentry stay green
