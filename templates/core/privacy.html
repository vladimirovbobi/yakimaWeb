Privacy Policy

Plain English. Last updated 2026-05-03.

Placeholder draft pending attorney review. Not legal advice. Will be replaced with reviewed version before public launch.

What we collect

  • Account info — your email address and password (hashed). Optional: full name, avatar, phone.
  • Realtor info — license number, license type, brokerage, headshot, bio. License number is a public record.
  • Activity — posts you write, comments you leave, leads you submit, AI tool runs.
  • Operational logs — IP address, user agent, page views (used for security + abuse detection, retained 12 months).
  • Moderation outcomes — every classification + human review action, retained for the life of your content for audit + appeals.

What we don't collect or do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't run ad-network trackers.
  • We don't share your email with third parties (other than transactional email + ARELLO for license verification).
  • We don't fingerprint you across sites.

How we use it

To provide the service: signups, license verification, posts, comments, AI tools, marketplace leads, moderation, security. That's it.

Third parties we share with

  • ARELLO — license number + name for verification (public record).
  • Postmark / email provider — to send you transactional email (verify, reset, notifications).
  • Cloudflare — hosts your uploaded media + protects the site from abuse.
  • Google Gemini API — content sent to our moderation classifier and AI tools (per Google's API terms).
  • Sentry — server error reports (no PII included).

Cookies

Session cookie for login (yw_sessionid). CSRF cookie for form security. No analytics cookies. No third-party tracking cookies.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of your data: privacy@yakimaweb.com.
  • Correction — edit your profile in your account.
  • Deletion — request account deletion via email. We'll remove your account; UGC may be anonymized rather than deleted to preserve thread integrity.
  • Export — portability via API + email request.

Security

HTTPS everywhere. Passwords hashed with Argon2 / PBKDF2. Two-factor authentication required for staff. IP allowlist on admin URLs. Audit log of every staff action.

Data retention

  • Account data: until you delete the account.
  • Operational logs (IP, UA): 12 months.
  • Moderation decisions: lifetime of the content + 12 months after deletion.
  • License verification audit trail: lifetime of your realtor profile + 7 years (industry-standard).

Children

Service is not directed at anyone under 18.

Changes

If we make material changes, we'll email you and post the diff here.

Contact

privacy@yakimaweb.com for any privacy question. Security issues to security@yakimaweb.com.
