About

A platform that respects your time.

Yakima Real Estate Hub exists because Central Washington realtors and homebuyers deserve a place that takes them seriously — and we noticed nobody was building it.

Most online real estate communities fall into one of two camps: walled-garden MLS portals you can't search without a login, or wide-open forums where anonymous posts compete with bots and out-of-state spam. Neither serves the actual work.

So we built the middle path. Verified realtors. Trusted local services. AI tools for the parts of the job that drain your day. A community where good answers beat loud ones — because real names + verified credentials + active moderation make that possible.

What we believe

Verification is a feature, not friction. Every realtor account is checked against the Washington Department of Licensing through ARELLO, at signup and every 30 days after. Suspended licenses lose the badge automatically. The trust signal stays honest.

Local matters more than scale. We chose Yakima, Kittitas, Benton, and Franklin counties on purpose. The bigger platforms don't know whether Selah or Tieton is the better commute for a Yakima nurse. We do.

AI tools should pull weight, not generate slop. Our furniture remover and description writer are tuned for real listings, not viral content. Use them on properties you represent. Per-user rate limits keep the costs sane and the output thoughtful.

Moderation is a craft, not a kill switch. Every post runs through a three-layer pipeline — deterministic checks, an AI classifier with prompt-injection defenses, and human review when it matters. Most posts publish in under a second. Borderline cases get a real person.

How we run

Independent. Not affiliated with any MLS, brokerage, or franchise. Built and operated from Yakima. Funded by featured listings and (eventually) vendor subscriptions in the marketplace — not by selling your data, never by selling your data, and not by serving you ads.

We log every moderation decision, every staff action, every license check. If we ever pull your post or downgrade your badge, you can ask why and we can show you exactly what happened.

Who we're for

  • Central Washington realtors — verified blog posts, marketplace presence, community questions, AI tools.
  • Service providers — photographers, lenders, junk removal, 3D tours, AI agents, automation, anything realtors hire. Lead-gen profiles in the marketplace.
  • Buyers + sellers — questions answered by people who actually know the local market. Reviews you can trust because they're tied to verified leads.

Get involved

Create an account to comment, ask questions, run AI tools, or follow realtors and vendors. If you're a Washington-licensed broker, submit your license to claim the verified badge. For service providers, the marketplace opens in our next phase — drop us a line at hello@yakimaweb.com to be among the first.

— The Yakima Real Estate Hub team
