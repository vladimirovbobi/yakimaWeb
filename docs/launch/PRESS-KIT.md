## What we are

Yakima Web is the Central Washington real estate hub. Verified-by-license
realtors publish market analysis. Local service vendors compete for leads
through transparent listings. Buyers and sellers explore the Valley through
AI-assisted listing tools and a moderated community forum. Built for the
Yakima Valley, run by Yakima Valley people.

## Key differentiators

- **License-verified realtors only.** Every realtor profile is checked against
  the ARELLO license database and re-checked every 30 days. No license, no
  blog post.
- **Lead-gen marketplace, not transactions.** Photographers, lenders, junk
  removal, drone tours, 3D walkthroughs — vendors compete for inquiries with
  transparent packages. We never hold money in escrow. Vendors and clients
  close deals directly.
- **AI tools that earn their place.** Furniture remover for vacant-room shots
  and a description writer trained on Yakima market vocabulary. Both run
  through a 3-layer moderation pipeline before output is shown.
- **Local focus.** Yakima, Selah, Naches, Wapato, Sunnyside, West Valley.
  No national listings, no Zillow scraping, no out-of-state spam.
